About me

I am an experienced CTO, Software Architect and Engineer with a Ph.D., MD, and BS in Computer Science from Genoa University, Italy.

Most recently, I have worked in a startup company called 28msec based in the Silicon Valley and Zurich for 4 years. There, I designed and developed distributed systems, query engines and data analytics software in C, C++, Java and functional languages.

In 28msec I also played a key role as project manager in the company's biggest project, that generated more revenue than all other projects in the company's history combined. The software developed in this project is being used, including in production, by central banks and system integrators all over the world.

Over the course of my career, I have discovered that I thrive on challenges and do my best work in highly demanding settings. I enjoy responsibility and believe that my experience, professionalism, and passion are a perfect fit for management, design, and development positions in the software industry.

I love to work with the latest technologies in order to design new products and features that have the power to revolutionize an industry. I am thus looking forward to companies which can give me a path to such a career.
